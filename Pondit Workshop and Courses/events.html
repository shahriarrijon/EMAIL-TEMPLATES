About Us

PONDIT aims to make skill-based training accessible and effective for the students of Bangladesh. As a renowned virtual training provider, we strive to equip learners with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in today's digital world. Our goal is to help the country progress toward becoming a developed nation.

Our Upcoming Workshop & Courses

We offer various trending technology-based courses and workshops at PONDIT Institute on a regular basis. Our upcoming courses include Fundamentals of Software Testing and Quality Assurance, The Complete Docker Course, Database Engineering for Efficient Backend Applications, and Spring by Practical Examples. Another workshop on the hottest topic "Data Science to Accelerate Business" is on the 3rd March 2023.


Our expert instructors and hands-on training can help you acquire valuable skills to advance your career. Please feel free to contact us for more information.
