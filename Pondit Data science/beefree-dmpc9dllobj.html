Data Science to Accelerate Business

4:00 - 8:00 PM
MARCH 3RD, 2023

Data Science has become a key component in most analytical and decision-making processes. The purpose of this workshop is to use data science and machine learning to make important business predictions or decisions based on the stored data. The program will feature 4 hands-on applications for Data Science, one for each of the following areas, (a) Strategic Business Analytics (b) Segmentation/Clustering (ML, AI) (c) Predictive Analytics (ML, AI). (d) Statistical Modeling, all of these hands-on will be focusing on solving real-life business problems.
